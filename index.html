The process of creating a roadmap for your future starts with highlighting themes you want to live your life by, which then drive your goals. These goals may be personal or professional. It is important to set long-term goals that are SMART:

  • Specific
  • Measurable
  • Attainable
  • Relevant
  • Time Bound

Together, your themes and goals can create a roadmap to help you guide your daily actions.
